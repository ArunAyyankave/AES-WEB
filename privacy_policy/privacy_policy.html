Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20/05/2025

Aesthetix Edutech ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://aesthetixedutech.com/ or interact with us through various channels, including our forms, online registrations, and advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide via contact forms, admission inquiries, newsletter subscriptions, and course registration forms.
  • Technical and Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent—collected automatically through cookies and tracking tools such as Meta Pixel and analytics software.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through WhatsApp or other chat integrations linked to our platform.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect data through:

  • Website contact and inquiry forms
  • Newsletter or email subscription forms
  • Course registration and enrollment forms
  • Meta Pixel and advertising tools
  • WhatsApp chat widgets or QR links
  • Cookies and analytics tracking

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide relevant information
  • Register you for courses and manage educational services
  • Send newsletters, updates, or promotional emails (you may unsubscribe anytime)
  • Run and improve our marketing campaigns on platforms like Meta (Facebook & Instagram)
  • Analyze website usage and improve functionality
  • Retarget users with relevant ads through Meta Ads and other advertising platforms

4. Sharing of Information

We may share data with trusted third parties, including:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc., for the purpose of running ads and analytics
  • Email marketing services (for newsletters or automated updates)
  • CRM or lead management tools used for admissions and student communications
  • WhatsApp Business tools used for support and communication

We do not sell your data to any third party.

5. Use of Cookies and Meta Pixel

Our website uses cookies and Meta Pixel to track visitor interactions. This helps us understand how users engage with our website and improve our advertising efforts. You may opt out of personalized ads using the links below:

  • Meta Ad Preferences
  • Your Online Choices

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Control cookie preferences via your browser settings

To make such a request, please contact us at info@aesthetixedutech.com.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

9. Third-party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
